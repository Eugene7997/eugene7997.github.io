3D printing exploration

March 27, 2026 Sharing

Introduction

I finally took the plunge and bought my first 3D printer. I wasn't entirely sure what I was getting myself into, but what could possibly go wrong?

Set up process

Unboxing

When the box arrived, the sheer size and complexity of the packaging felt pretty intimidating.

With over SGD$600 at stake, I definitely didn't want to carelessly tear into it. Thankfully, an official unboxing video from Bambu Labs served as a helpful guide. There were some slight differences from my actual unit that gave me pause here and there, but nothing a few breaks and a little guesswork couldn't handle.

Set up

The physical assembly was a bit challenging. Keep in mind that the official Bambu Labs unboxing video feels a bit outdated, so I had to rely heavily on the provided written manual to navigate the finer, updated details.

One critical detail the video missed was a warning about a specific wire prone to getting squashed during assembly. If I hadn't caught it in time, the cable could have been silently damaged beyond repair.

But after a full afternoon of careful tinkering and hard work, the setup was finally complete.

Nice add ons

Along the way, I discovered some fantastic mods to significantly upgrade the 3D printer's functionality. For example:

  • Bambu lab a1 ams lite top mount helped save space which I crucially need.
  • Bambu lab a1 wire holders helped prevent damage to wires and keep them tidy.
  • Screen protector that proved useful when my spool fell off.
  • Poop collector that proves invaluable in keeping mess to a minimum.
  • A fan guard in case my fingers get caught up.
  • Top cube enclosure that keeps dust off the 3D printer and protects it.

Review

I regretted making this purchase. Had I know how fun and useful 3D printing is, I would have spent just $300 more to buy a Bambu labs P1S with AMS combo. Trying to find a enclosure that fits the A1 combo has been time consuming along with the numerous mods. Plus it takes more space, and I can't print ABS.
