You must be wondering why this website is so simple and barebones.
During my earlier years where I was interested in investing, I used to visit alot of company websites for information. However, none surprised me more than Berkshire Hathaway's website.
It was simple, clean, and easy to navigate.

Amongst other reasons, I wanted to challenge myself to create a website without using any modern frameworks and as a 1999 kid, I wanted to pay homage to the old school websites.
I hope you like it! And if you don't, maybe others can convince you otherwise.
